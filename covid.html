Deep Cleaning

We apply a cleaning solution to remove surface contaminants acting as the top layer of a particular area. Using water and cleaning fluid to wipe away the grime results in a thorough cleanse of the surface which will counteract any potentially dangerous pathogens. Absorbing the remaining water and disinfectant solution ensures the area is safe for regular usage once again.

Disinfectant Services

Once your space has been properly cleaned, our trained technicians apply a hospital-grade disinfectant called Hypochlorous acid. This disinfectant is effective against a wide range of pathogens, including the bacteria and germs that spread COVID-19. When used at the right concentrations it is also one of the safest products available on the market for cleaning food preparation areas, educational facilities and more.

Antimicrobial Coating

For those who want to be absolutely certain that all surfaces are reinforced against germs, we provide a technological solution based on a unique antimicrobial coating that protects you long after we’re gone. The coating remains attached to the surface even after your regular daily cleaning products are applied and leaves absolutely no physical residue. Our team has the experience and expertise necessary to provide high grade disinfectant services that will give your staff and residents peace of mind. Our unique medical cleaning package includes deep cleaning that reduces cross-contamination for medical office buildings, hospitals, senior living facilities and outpatient centers.
